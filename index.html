APPLE

APPLE SCAB

Coused By:The ascomycete fungus Venturia inaequalis.

Symptoms: Twisted and Puckered leaves that have black, circular scabby spots on the underside.

GRAPES

GRAPE BLACK ROT

Coused By:The fungus Botryosphaeria obtusa.

Symptoms:They appear as small, purple specks on the upper surface of the leaves that enlarge into circular lesions 1/8 to 1/4 inch (3-6 mm) in diameter.

TOMATO

Bacterial spot

Coused By:Xanthomonas vesicatoria, Xanthomonas euvesicatoria, Xanthomonas gardneri, and Xanthomonas perforans.

Symptoms:Leaf spots turn from yellow or light green to black or dark brown. Older spots are black, slightly raised, superficial.

TOMATO

Tomato Early Blight

Coused By:Alternaria tomatophila and Alternaria solani.

Symptoms:Leaf spots turn from yellow or light green to black or dark brown. Older spots are black, slightly raised, superficial and measure up to 0.3 inch (7.5 mm) in diameter.

TOMATO

tomato late blight

Coused By:A fungus-like microorganism that infects and kills tomato and potato plants.

Symptoms:In cool, wet weather, entire fields turn brown and wilted as if hit by frost. In tomatoes, firm, dark brown, circular spots grow to cover large parts of fruits.

TOMATO

Tomato Mold

Coused By: a fungal pathogen called Passalora fulva (syn. Cladosporium fulvum).

Symptoms:Symptoms start as pale green to yellowish spots on upper leaf surfaces that turn a bright yellow.

TOMATO

tomato septoria leaf spot

Coused By: The fungus Septoria lycopersici.

Symptoms: Small, water-soaked, circular spots 1/16 to 1/8" in diameter on the undersides of older leaves

TOMATO

Tomato spider mites

Coused By:Hot and dry conditions

Symptoms:Pale-orange to red and feed on the underside of leaves.

TOMATO

Tomato target spot

Coused By:Pathogen Corynespora cassiicola

Symptoms:Some of the spots enlarge up to 10 mm and show characteristics rings

TOMATO

Tomato mosaic virus

Coused By:Plant pathogenic virus like Virgaviridae Tobamovirus.

Symptoms:The foliage of affected tomato plants shows mottling, with alternating yellowish and darker green areas

TOMATO

Tomato yellow leaf curl virus

Coused By:Silverleaf whitefly (Bemisia tabaci)

Symptoms:Severe stunting, reduction of leaf size, upward cupping/curling of leaves

Grapes esca
